Who are we?

At Stretch Yoga Studio, we wholeheartedly believe that yoga changes the way you feel, look and how you show up in the world. Beyond its promise to tone & strengthen your body and increase flexibility, it can also inspire you to slow down, connect to self and cultivate mindfulness. You’ll love our tranquil space, class variety and unrivalled infrared heating experience. ​Our friendly teachers are authentic, gentle souls who encourage you on your journey. Join us for hot, warm and non-heated yoga and pilates classes in a friendly, supportive, non-competitive environment.

The world is forever changed because of the pandemic and we are committed to doing our part to keep our community as safe as possible. Masks are mandatory to enter the studio and must be worn in common areas when physical activity is not in progress. You may remove your mask once you are on your mat We maintain strict cleaning protocols and ask you to take responsibility for your own health and wellbeing. We insist that you refrain from entering the studio for at least 2 weeks if you feel unwell, exhibit any Covid-19 symptoms, have been travelling out of country or have been in contact with anyone who has Covid-19. Register for classes in advance and pay for passes online for touchless transactions. We will continue to stay up to date with new information as it becomes available and will amend our policies and procedures as necessary.
